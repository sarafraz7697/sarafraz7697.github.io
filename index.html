About

From the age of 14, I’ve been deeply passionate about software engineering and problem-solving. My journey began with a simple yet powerful interest in World of Warcraft. Along with my friends, I dove into the world of online gaming, where we decided to create our own private server. Back then, we didn’t know much about coding, but with determination and a lot of research, we discovered the magic of repacks for World of Warcraft servers.

Although I had no experience in Lua, the scripting language used for WoW servers, we began experimenting. Through testing, troubleshooting, and learning as we went, we were able to create our first Lua script that showed a simple in-game menu with teleporting options, a basic buy center, and other features. This was my first taste of coding, and it was exhilarating to modify game mechanics, write scripts, and even interact with MySQL databases—without knowing much about programming or databases at the time!

But we weren’t satisfied with just modifying the repack. We wanted to build our own server from scratch, customizing everything from the code itself. This led us to explore open-source World of Warcraft servers. It felt overwhelming at first, but we persevered, learning how to build the C++ server from source using Visual Studio. I had no formal knowledge of C++—I simply dove into the code, made changes, debugged errors, and resolved issues through trial and error.

Through this hands-on, problem-solving experience, I developed a deep interest in programming and software engineering. The thrill of making things work, overcoming challenges, and continuously learning sparked a passion that continues to drive me today.

As I grew older, I pursued a Bachelor’s degree in Software Engineering from Montazeri Mashhad University. Over the years, I gained experience working for various companies, developing backend systems, APIs, and microservices. Today, I’m a Senior Software Engineer specializing in:

  • Backend Development (Node.js, Golang)
  • REST APIs & Microservices
  • Agile Methodologies
  • Docker and Containerization

My journey, from modifying game servers to building scalable software solutions, has been shaped by persistence, curiosity, and a passion for continuous learning. I’m excited to keep growing, solving complex problems, and creating impactful solutions in the world of backend development.
